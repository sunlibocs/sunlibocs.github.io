About Me

Dr. Sun is currently a researcher at The University of Adelaide. Over the past six years, he has studied and worked at the Australian Centre for Robotic Vision, The University of Adelaide (working with Prof. Chunhua Shen), and Nanyang Technological University in Singapore. His main research areas are robotics and artificial intelligence, specifically including, but not limited to, depth estimation, image segmentation, 3D reconstruction, visual navigation, and visualization. His papers have been published in top-tier journals and conferences such as T-PAMI in artificial intelligence, T-RO in robotics, and CVPR in computer vision. He has served as a reviewer for almost all top-tier journals and conferences related to robotic vision.

Dr. Sun 现为澳大利亚机器学习研究所研究员。 此前六年在澳大利亚机器人视觉中心、阿德莱德大学、新加坡南洋理工大学学习和工作。 主要研究方向为机器人学及人工智能，具体包括但不限于，深度估计、图像分割、3D重建、视觉导航、可视化。 相关论文发表于人工智能顶刊T-PAMI、机器人顶刊T-RO、机器视觉顶会CVPR等，担任几乎所有机器视觉相关顶刊/顶会的审稿人。
