About Me

Dr. Sun is a researcher in the field of robotics and machine learning. Over the past several years, he has studied and worked at the Australian Centre for Robotic Vision (PhD affiliated with ACRV Adelaide), The University of Adelaide (working with Prof. Chunhua Shen), and Nanyang Technological University (Research Associate). His main research interests are robotics and machine learning, specifically including, but not limited to, depth estimation, 3D reconstruction, neural rendering, and robot manipulation. His papers have been published in top-tier journals and conferences such as T-PAMI in artificial intelligence, T-RO in robotics, and CVPR in computer vision. He has served as a reviewer for almost all top-tier journals and conferences related to robotic vision.
